About SudoSLVRR

The Research

SudoSLVRR is more than just a game. It is another step for innovating faster and reliable algorithms. The system was developed under the thesis title: SudoSLVRR: A Web-based Sudoku Solver using Multithreaded Multi-Colony Ant Optimization with Ring and Random Communication Topologies.

By mimicking the biological foraging behavior of ants through artificial pheromone trails and utilizing parallel computing threads, the engine is designed to rapidly crack not just standard 9x9 puzzles, but massive, high-order 16x16 and 25x25 grids.

Can You Beat the Ants? 🐜

Think your logic is faster than a highly optimized swarm intelligence? We challenge you to find out! Jump into Game Mode and try to solve the grid manually using your own brainpower.

If you get completely stuck, simply hit the Solve button. The engine will deploy its digital ant colonies onto the board, seamlessly communicating and laying down pheromones to find the correct configuration in seconds. Try to beat the ants before they beat the board!

Key Features

  • Multiple Grid Sizes: Play and solve standard 9x9 puzzles, or challenge yourself with massive 16x16 and 25x25 extended grids.
  • Interactive Game Mode: A clean UI for manual solving, complete with real-time error checking (duplicate highlights) and undo/erase tools.
  • Advanced Experiment Mode: Full access to the engine's parameters. Tweak the number of ants, evaporation rates, convergence thresholds, and thread communication intervals to see how the AI reacts.
  • Custom Puzzle Creation: Build your own impossible puzzles from scratch, save them to the library, or upload them via standard .txt files.
  • PDF Exporting: Generate clean, 2-page printable PDF documents containing both the initial puzzle state and the final SudoSLVRR solution.
